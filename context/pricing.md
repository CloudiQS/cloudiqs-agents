# CloudiQS Pricing

## Service pricing

### MSP (Managed AWS)
Ongoing management, monitoring, cost optimisation, patching, on-call support.

| Tier | Monthly | What's included |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| Starter | £2,000 | Up to 10 resources, business hours support, monthly cost report |
| Growth | £4,500 | Up to 30 resources, extended hours, weekly cost report, WAR review annually |
| Scale | £7,500 | Up to 75 resources, 24/7 on-call, weekly review, quarterly architecture review |
| Enterprise | £10,000+ | Unlimited resources, dedicated engineer, SLA-backed, monthly exec review |

**Positioning note:** AWS cost savings alone typically offset MSP fees within 60 days. Lead with this.

---

### Migration (lift-and-shift or re-architect)
Fixed-scope project to move workloads from on-prem, VMware, Azure, or GCP to AWS.

| Workload size | Price range | Notes |
|--------------|-------------|-------|
| Small (1–5 servers, <50TB) | £15,000–£30,000 | Single environment, standard stack |
| Medium (5–20 servers, 50–200TB) | £30,000–£55,000 | Multi-environment, some re-architecture |
| Large (20+ servers, 200TB+) | £55,000–£80,000 | Full re-architecture, parallel run, cutover plan |

**MAP funding:** Qualifying migrations can receive AWS credits covering 25–35% of project cost. For most customers this is £15K–£45K. We handle the MAP application.

## AWS funding available

| Programme | What it covers | Typical value |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| MAP (Migration Acceleration Program) | Migration project costs | £15K–£80K AWS credits |
| Well-Architected Review | WAR assessment | Free (up to 2 days) |
| ISV Workload Migration | ISV-specific migration | Project-specific |
| POC Credits | Proof-of-concept work | £5K–£20K |

**How to qualify:** Any company migrating workloads to AWS with 20+ VMs or 50TB+ data is likely MAP-eligible. We do the paperwork. The credit comes off the AWS invoice — not ours.

## Pricing rules for agents

- Never quote a price in a cold email — always lead with value and a meeting
- If asked for ballpark, give a range: "typically £X–£Y depending on scope"
- Always mention MAP funding before any price conversation
- Do not discount. If budget is the objection, go to MAP funding first
- Agentic Bakery is not sold on first contact — requires discovery session first
