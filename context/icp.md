# CloudiQS ICP — Ideal Customer Profile

## Primary ICP

**UK SMBs and mid-market companies migrating to or scaling on AWS.**

| Dimension | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Geography | UK registered (Companies House) |
| Size | 50–500 employees |
| Revenue | £5M–£150M ARR |
| AWS spend | £2K–£10K/month (or pre-migration) |
| Decision maker | CTO, IT Director, Head of Infrastructure, VP Engineering |
| Budget cycle | Q1 (Jan–Mar) and Q3 (Jul–Sep) strongest |

## Vertical priorities (in order)

1. **VMware/Broadcom exits** — companies running VMware post-Broadcom acquisition, facing 3–5x licence increases. Strongest buying signal now.
2. **MSP/IT services** — managed service providers needing cloud delivery capability for their own customers
3. **Funded startups** — Seed to Series B, 12–18 months post-funding, infrastructure scaling pain
4. **Financial services** — fintech, wealth management, insurance — SOC2 / FCA compliance pressure
5. **Manufacturing / industrial** — legacy on-prem, ERP migration, IoT data
6. **Education** — MATs, universities, EdTech platforms, Microsoft/Google exit
7. **Media / creative** — render farms, storage-heavy workloads, FSx
8. **Security-first buyers** — post-breach, compliance audit, CISO-led decisions

## Buying signals (high intent)

- Job ads: "AWS", "Cloud Engineer", "DevOps", "Platform Engineer", "Site Reliability"
- Tech news: "migrating to cloud", "digital transformation", "infrastructure modernisation"
- Companies House: filed accounts in last 12 months, active status, no dormant flag
- LinkedIn activity: CTO/IT Director posting about cloud, AWS, or infrastructure challenges
- Funding announcement (Crunchbase/Companies House): new round in last 6 months
- VMware licensing renewal due (inferred from headcount + on-prem signals)
- AWS funding eligibility: MAP, Well-Architected Review, ISV Workload Migration

## Disqualifications (skip immediately)

- Recruiters, staffing agencies
- IT resellers: CDW, Softcat, Computacenter, Bytes, SHI
- Cloud providers or AWS partners (competitors)
- Consultancies over 500 employees
- Sole traders or fewer than 10 employees
- Dormant companies on Companies House
- Outside UK (no Companies House record)

## ICP scoring (6+ required to proceed)

| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| UK registered, active on Companies House | +2 |
| 50–500 employees confirmed | +1 |
| Named IT decision maker found | +1 |
| Clear AWS migration or scaling pain | +2 |
| VMware or on-prem signal | +1 |
| Funded in last 6 months | +1 |
| Compliance pressure (SOC2, FCA, GDPR) | +1 |
| Job ad for cloud/DevOps role | +1 |

**Score 6–7:** proceed with standard outreach
**Score 8–10:** priority — escalate to `sdr-account-intel` for deep research

## Budget indicators

- Companies with £10M+ revenue and an active IT team likely have budget
- Funded startups: assume 10–15% of round available for infrastructure in year 1
- MSPs: look for companies billing clients £500K+ in managed services (implies AWS cost)
- Manufacturing: ERP migration projects often £50K–£200K; infrastructure is a line item
